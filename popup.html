A 30-second tour so you know what to do.

Step 1: Copy a JD

Open any job posting (LinkedIn, Seek, Indeed, company sites...).

Select the job description text and copy it (Cmd/Ctrl+C).

🔍

Step 2: Paste & Analyze

Paste the JD into the text box, then click Analyze.

You'll get a match score, keywords, strengths, and skill gaps.

✨
